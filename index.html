Pete's Coffee

Our Story

Pete's Coffee was established in 2019 as a friendly neighbourhood coffeeshop. Instead of weird names and unclear drink descriptions, we wanted to ensure that Pete's Coffee was accessible and understandable for the average person. Simple names and simple explanations are presented as you walk into the door. Our goal is to make you feel comfortable and confident in your brewing selections and offer drinks from the common to the connisseur level.

Stop on by, we'd love to meet you!
